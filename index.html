Importnce of agriculture

Food Security: Agriculture is the primary source of food production. food importance It ensures a stable food supply for the growing population, reducing the dependency on food imports and mitigating the risk of hunger and malnutrition.

Employment: Agriculture is a significant source of employment, especially in rural areas where the majority of the population resides. It provides livelihoods for millions of farmers, farmworkers, and agribusinesses involved in various stages of the agricultural value chain.

Economic Growth: Agriculture contributes to the overall economic growth of a country. It generates income for farmers, stimulates rural economies, and provides raw materials for agro-based industries, thereby fostering industrialization and economic diversification.

Foreign Exchange Earnings: Agricultural exports, such as cocoa, palm oil, and cashew nuts, contribute to foreign exchange earnings, which are essential for balancing trade deficits, supporting the economy, and financing imports of essential goods and services.
